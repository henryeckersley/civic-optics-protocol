Civic Optics Protocol (COP‑1)

The Civic Optics Protocol is an open standard for assessing the quality of public‑facing civic information. It helps advocates, researchers and public servants evaluate whether information is legible, understandable, navigable, current and actionable.

Standard Sample audit README

Why COP‑1?

Democracy depends on accessible information. Elections, public meetings and policy changes are often published in formats that are hard to find or understand. COP‑1 offers a simple, replicable way to measure and improve the quality of these materials.

How it works

  • Assign scores (0–10) to each criterion based on the rubric defined in protocol.md.
  • Use the weighting formula to compute the Civic Optics Index (COI).
  • Publish your results and recommendations to encourage improvement.

Check out the sample audit for an example and the scorer.py script for a command‑line tool to calculate scores.
