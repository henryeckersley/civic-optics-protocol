COP-1 Calculator

This page is the actual tool. Score a public information page from 0 to 10 across the five categories and it calculates the final COP-1 score automatically.

0 = terrible, 10 = excellent. Weights: Legibility 20%, Understandability 30%, Navigability 20%, Currency 15%, Actionability 15%.

Interpretation
85-100 = excellent
70-84 = strong
55-69 = usable but flawed
below 55 = hard for the public to use

Use Copy report after scoring a page, then paste the result into a doc, spreadsheet, email, or article draft.

How to use it

1. Open a public page you want to audit.
2. Move the five sliders based on how usable the page feels.
3. Read the total score and copy the generated report.
